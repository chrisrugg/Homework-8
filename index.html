About Me

My name is Chris Rugg. I was born in Red Bank NJ and currently live in Highlands NJ. I have two sisters and two brothers that live withing ten miles of me. I enjoy fishing, hiking and reading. I attend Saint Georges Episcopal church in Rumson NJ. I volunteer for my best friends charity Hops for Awareness. This charity brings beverage industry professionals together at various charity events to raise money to help disabled adults especially adults with Autism.

I currently work at SkuNexus a warehouse software development firm in Highland Park NJ. SkuNexus develops warehouse manageent software that integrates with Shopify, Big Commerce and Magento. I spent most of my career at AT&T in Atlanta and Dallas. While at AT&T I worked on many software projects as a project and product manager.
